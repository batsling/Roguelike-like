🎮 How to Play

Goal: Enter the Dungeon of Roguelikes, grab the Amulet of Nivlac, and escape without dying.

🗺️ Exploration Phase

  • Navigate a map of roguelikes connected through inspiration
  • Move one game at a time across the map
  • After beating a game, you get a random item choice
  • Then you trigger the space's Encounter:
    • ! = Enemy Combat (dice roll using your stats)
    • ? = Event (story choice)
    • $ = Shop (buy items with gold)
  • The encounter type is shown as an icon in the top-right of each space
  • Find the game marked with 🏺 - that's where the Amulet is!

🏃 Escape Phase

  • Once you beat the Amulet game, you enter the Escape Phase
  • Choose 3 games you've already beaten to replay
  • For each game, you'll see two buttons:
    • Lost Run - Click this each time you lose a run while playing that game (-1 health per loss)
    • Finished - Click this when you beat the game to progress to the next one
  • Complete all 3 games without your health reaching 0 to escape with the Amulet!

😈 Curses System

  • Curses are debuffs with three tiers (I, II, III) that get stronger each tier
  • Some curses are automatic (start of run), others trigger on events or combat failures
  • Curses have durations: track how many uses/games/spaces before they expire
  • View your curses collection in the 📚 Collection menu

🎯 Bingo Challenges

  • Complete bingo goals by clicking cells in the 3x3 grid on the right
  • Goals have three difficulties: Easy, Normal, Hard
  • Complete a row, column, or diagonal to earn rewards in order
  • All rewards give +1 to all combat stats plus items and bonuses
  • Click Rewards to see what each bingo completion grants

🗺️ Map Viewer

  • Click View Map to see the full roguelike influence graph
  • Use +/- buttons to zoom in/out on the map
  • Games are color-coded: visited, available, locked
  • Hover over games to see shortest path distances

📚 Collection Menu

  • Games: See all roguelikes, their connections, and stats (beaten count, amulets collected)
  • Items: Browse all items by rarity (Common, Uncommon, Rare)
  • Enemies: View all enemies and their stats
  • Curses: Review all curses with tier tabs (I, II, III)

💡 Tip: Build up health and items during exploration before grabbing the Amulet!
