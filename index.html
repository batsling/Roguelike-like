🎮 How to Play

Goal: Navigate a dungeon built from real roguelikes, fight through encounters at each location, find the Amulet of Nivlac, and escape alive.

🗺️ The Map

  • The dungeon is a web of roguelike games connected by their real-world inspiration links
  • Click any adjacent, unlocked game node to move there — you can only move one step at a time
  • Clicking a node opens a preview card showing the enemies you'll face and the two post-combat options waiting for you if you win
  • Click Fight in the preview to enter the encounter
  • Find the node marked 🏺 — that's where the Amulet is hidden
  • Use 🗺️ Map in the top bar to see the full graph at any time

⚔️ Encounters

Each node has one encounter type shown as an icon:

  • ⚔️ Combat — Fight one or more enemies. You'll use either card combat or dice combat depending on the game. Win to collect gold, loot, and post-combat rewards
  • ? Event — A story situation. Most choices require a stat check: roll a D20, add your relevant stat, and beat the target number. Higher stats mean better odds. Results range from critical success to critical failure
  • $ Shop — Spend gold on items. Charisma gives a small discount. You can reroll the stock (first reroll is free)

🃏 Card Combat

  • Draw cards from your deck each turn and play them by spending Energy (2 per turn by default)
  • Cards deal damage, apply block, inflict status effects, or do both
  • Upgraded cards (shown with + green text) have improved values
  • Remove or upgrade cards between fights to thin your deck and increase consistency

🎲 Dice Combat

  • Each turn you roll your character die and weapon die — each face has a move (Damage, Block, Heal, etc.)
  • Select which rolled faces to use; each move costs 1 Energy
  • Some faces are Spells that cost Mana instead — you have up to 3 Mana per combat
  • Status effects like Burn, Poison, Stun, and Dodge carry over between turns

📊 Stats

  • Strength → every 3 points = +1 Power (bonus damage on attacks)
  • Dexterity → every 3 points = +1 Defense (bonus block)
  • Intelligence → every 3 points = +1 Arcane (bonus magic damage)
  • Charisma → every 5 points = +1 Persistence (buff/debuff intensity) + shop discounts
  • Stats also determine your bonus to event stat checks — higher is always better

🏆 After Combat

  • Winning a combat shows a victory screen with gold, loot, and optionally a new card
  • Then you choose one of two post-combat options (pre-assigned when you previewed the node):
    • 🛌 Rest — Heal 33% of your max health
    • ⚒️ Smith — Choose 2 cards from your deck to upgrade for free
    • 🛒 Shop — Open a fresh shop visit
    • 🗺️ Movement Event — A special navigation reward

🎒 Loot

  • Click 🎒 Loot in the top bar to open your loot at any time
  • Scrolls — Use outside of combat. Two D20 rolls determine the outcome (crit success / success / failure / crit failure). Intelligence improves your odds. Click a scroll in the Collection to see its possible outcomes
  • Potions — Use during combat only. Click a potion, then click a target (or drag it onto one). You won't know what a potion does until you use it for the first time this run
  • Fish — Caught from special fishing locations. Sell them in the shop for gold

📈 Difficulty Progression

  • Every 4 games beaten fills one segment of the difficulty battery and increases the tier
  • Tiers: Easy (0–3) → Medium (4–7) → Hard (8–11) → Insane (12+)
  • Higher tiers bring stronger enemies, harder event checks, and harder DC rolls
  • At Insane, filling each battery segment causes an Overheat: you gain escalating curses and a hard enemy is queued for your next combat

😈 Curses

  • Curses are debuffs that range from mild to crippling across three tiers (I, II, III)
  • They come from event failures, combat penalties, and Insane-tier overheats
  • Curses expire after a set number of games, uses, or spaces — track them in the 📚 Collection

🎯 Bingo

  • A 3×3 grid of optional challenge goals sits on the right side of the screen
  • Mark a goal complete by clicking its cell; goals have Easy, Normal, and Hard difficulties
  • Complete a row, column, or diagonal to earn a reward — each reward grants stat bonuses, items, or run modifiers

🏃 Escape Phase

  • Once you beat the Amulet node, the Escape Phase begins
  • Choose 3 games you've already visited to replay on your way out
  • For each game, actually go play it. Then record the result:
    • Lost Run — Press this every time you die in that game (−1 health each)
    • Finished — Press this when you beat the game to advance
  • If your health hits 0 during the escape, you lose. Survive all 3 to win!

📚 Collection

  • Games — All roguelikes in the dungeon, their connections, and your history with them
  • Items — Every item by rarity; hover to read effects
  • Enemies — All enemies and their stats
  • Curses — All curses across all tiers; click active curses to see full descriptions
  • Scrolls — Click any scroll to see its four possible outcomes before using it

💡 Tip: The node preview shows you exactly which enemies are waiting and which two post-combat options are on offer — use that information to plan your route before committing to a fight.
